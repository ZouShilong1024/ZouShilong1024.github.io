Shilong Zou （邹世龙）

Email · GitHub · Rednote · Photography
I am currently pursuing the master's degree at the National University of Defense Technology advised by Prof. Kai Xu and Prof. Chenyang Zhu. I received the bachelor's degree from the University of Dalian Maritime University, Dalian, 2023.
My research focuses on Embodied AI (including WFM, VLA, and RL), Computer Vision, and Deep Learning.
We are actively exploring new ideas to create intelligent robotic systems capable of learning dexterous, human-like behaviors and enhancing human life, while also committed to building a universal world model.
